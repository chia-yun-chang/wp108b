Siberian Husky:

The eyes of Siberian Husky are usually brown, light brown or blue. Light blue winks are a common wink, but there are exceptions. A dog may have one eye brown or light brown, while the other is blue, this phenomenon is called "bi-eyed"; or one eye is blue and the iris of the other eye is mixed The colored, iris heterochromia, is called "parti-eyed" by lovers of Siberian huskies. Among the dog breeds commonly supported by people, the Siberian Husky is a rare breed with two eyes that can have different colors. At the same time, it is also one of the few dog breeds whose eyes are generally blue.
