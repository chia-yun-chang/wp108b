Miniature Schnauzer Terrier:

Miniature Schnauzer is a small dog breed in the Schnauzer family, which was produced in Germany in the middle and late 19th century. The Miniature Schnauzer is derived from the standard Schnauzer and many small dogs, including poodles, miniature Pinscher or Effing Doberman breeds. Miniature Schnauzer is most familiar with their friendly personality and naughty and full of energy, they are very loyal and protective of the owner.
